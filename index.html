Code Studio helps students at Sint Lucas Antwerp with their questions about the digital medium.

Questions like:

  • How do I make a portfolio website?
  • How do I make an installaction that reacts to sound?
  • How do I make an interactive world in virtual reality?

Code Studio can support you in many domains:

  • VR: 3D painting, 360 video serious games
  • Digital fabrication: laser cutting, 3D printing
  • Physical computing: drawing robots, IOT
  • Web: interactive media, portfolio
  • AI: image classification, text generation, image generation

Are you a bachelor or (pre)master student at Sint Lucas? We can help you with the whole process: from helping you figure out what's possible and attainable to the technical implementation, to the final presentation.
