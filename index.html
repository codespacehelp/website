Code Space is the digital workshop of Sint Lucas Antwerp. We help bachelor and master students with their questions about the digital medium. We can help you with the whole process: from helping you figure out what's possible and attainable to the technical implementation, to the final presentation. Code Space can support you in many domains:

  • VR: 3D painting, 360 video serious games
  • Digital fabrication: laser cutting, 3D printing, pen plotting
  • Physical computing: drawing robots, IOT
  • Web: interactive media, portfolio
  • AI: image classification, text generation, image generation

To use the machines and arrange a meeting with one of the tutors, please make a reservation through the link below. An email will be sent to you. The workshop is also open during the opening hours mentioned below, but it can be busy, so it's prefferable to make a reservation.

Please check the material dimensions and file formats you need for each machine.
